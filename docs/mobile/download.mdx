## Cyd for iOS is Under Review by Apple

Apple, an American corporation with [close ties](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/11/trump-ceos-elon-musk-tim-cook-larry-fink-xi-china-summit.html) to the US government, retains a monopoly on what software iPhone users are allowed to run on their own devices. It [has](https://www.404media.co/iceblock-owner-after-apple-removes-app-we-are-determined-to-fight-this/) [abused](https://www.404media.co/apple-banned-an-app-that-simply-archived-videos-of-ice-abuses/) this monopoly in the past. App developers like us need to submit our apps for review, and wait for Apple's blessing, before they're allowed to appear in the App Store.

Cyd for iPhone is currently under review. Apple seems to not like the fact that our premium features don't use In-App Purchases, so therefore they can't take their [15-30% cut](https://appleinsider.com/articles/23/01/08/the-cost-of-doing-business-apples-app-store-fees-explained).

Until then, the mobile version of Cyd is just available for Android.
